About Cosmic Vanguard

Cosmic Vanguard is an integrated research platform that brings together multiple state-of-the-art machine learning approaches for astronomical analysis. Built for researchers, universities, and institutes working at the frontier of observational astrophysics.

Each module represents a distinct research contribution, addressing real-world challenges in galaxy evolution, quasar variability, and stellar population synthesis using advanced neural network architectures.
